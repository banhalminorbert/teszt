About

Bognár Katalin Photography

Bognár Katalin Photography is a Vienna–Győr focused portrait brand for private clients, couples, families and entrepreneurs. The brand is built on hospitality-driven empathy: listening, creating safety, setting a calm rhythm and turning the portrait experience into something natural rather than stressful.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

I am not photogenic. Is that a problem?

No. The session is designed to help you feel safe and natural with gentle guidance.

What should I wear?

Wear something comfortable. Clean lines and calm colours usually work better than strong patterns.

Where will the session take place?

In Vienna, Győr or an agreed location based on light, purpose and comfort.

How long does it take?

It depends on the scope. The focus is a calm rhythm, not rushing.

When will I receive the images?

Delivery timing is defined in the quote.

Is retouching included?

Natural retouching can be included without changing who you are.

Can I use the images on LinkedIn?

Yes, business portraits are made for LinkedIn, websites and professional profiles.

Can my photos appear in the portfolio?

Only with separate written consent.

Can I ask for full privacy?

Yes. Non-public use can be agreed in writing.

What about children?

Parental or legal guardian consent is required; publication needs separate permission.

Do couples need to pose?

No. The goal is natural connection, not stiff posing.

What if the weather is bad?

We arrange another time or an indoor alternative.

How can I cancel?

Cancellation terms are defined in the quote or shooting terms.

How does payment work?

Payment method and timing are stated in the quote.

Which languages are available?

Hungarian, German and English.

Do you work in Vienna and Győr?

The focus is Vienna, Lower Austria, Győr and the Győr-Moson-Sopron region.

What is a model release?

A written permission that defines how images may be used or published.

How is my data protected?

Contact data is processed only for enquiry and quotation purposes.

How long are images stored?

Retention should be defined in the contract or shooting terms.

What makes the session natural?

Clear preparation, calm direction and no forced posing.
